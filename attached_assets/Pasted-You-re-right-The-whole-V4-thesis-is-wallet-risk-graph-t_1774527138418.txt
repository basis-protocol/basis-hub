The wallet risk graph should track all token exposure, not just stablecoins. A whale with $50M in WBTC has real concentration risk even though WBTC isn’t a stablecoin.

	1.	Sample the top 200 wallets by total_stablecoin_value from the previous run.
	2.	For each, call fetch_token_list() to get recent ERC-20 transfer history.
	3.	Extract unique contract addresses not already in the known list.
	4.	For each new contract, store basic metadata (symbol, name, decimals from the tokentx response) into wallet_graph.unscored_assets via upsert_unscored_asset().

	∙	Stablecoin (symbol contains USD/DAI/FRAX patterns, or CoinGecko category = stablecoin) → eligible for SII scoring promotion via the existing flywheel

	7.	Include all discovered contracts in the current run’s batch scan so their balances get recorded in wallet_holdings.
